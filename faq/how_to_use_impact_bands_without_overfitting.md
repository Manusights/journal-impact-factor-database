# How to Use Impact Bands Without Overfitting

Impact bands help with segmentation, not final decisions.

Use impact band as one column in a multi-factor decision table. Never rank by impact factor alone.


## Why this matters
A consistent process reduces avoidable journal mismatch and rework.
