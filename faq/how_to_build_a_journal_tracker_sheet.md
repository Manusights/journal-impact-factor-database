# How to Build a Journal Tracker Sheet

Recommended columns:
- Journal name
- Scope fit score
- Quartile
- Impact band
- Submission status
- Decision date
- Notes

Update weekly during active submission cycles.


## Why this matters
A consistent process reduces avoidable journal mismatch and rework.
