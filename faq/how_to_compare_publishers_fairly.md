# How to Compare Publishers Fairly

Do not compare by prestige language.

Compare by:
- Scope fit
- Policy transparency
- Submission workflow clarity
- Historical alignment with your manuscript type


## Why this matters
A consistent process reduces avoidable journal mismatch and rework.
