# What to Do When Impact Factor Is Missing

Treat it as unknown, not low quality by default.

Actions:
- Verify current indexing status
- Check publisher and editorial board quality
- Use field relevance and readership as primary criteria


## Why this matters
A consistent process reduces avoidable journal mismatch and rework.
